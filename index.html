I Promise to Fight

I will fight for Religious Freedom.

As a member of the Asatru religion, I will fight against religious nationalism in our country, upholding the First of the Five parts in the First Amendment of the United States Constitution.

I will fight for Transparency, Integrity, and Constitution Enforcement.

For too many years, criminals and bad actors of the law enforcement, judges and lower politicians, matter how high the crimes have been getting away scott free while the citizens have had to pay the prices for all the lawlessness while we are required to follow the laws as good citizens, I have created an organization called O.N.C.E. just waiting for congressional constitutional enforcement powers. 

I will fight to legalize & remove restrictions on Non/Less lethal Self Defense Arms.

While illegally obtained guns are in the hands of so many criminals, Members of congress and state house of representatives saw it fit to disarm the law abided citizens of their lawfully owned Arms leaving the citizens helpless against the armed criminals, although the excuses are gun violence, they've also deemed it fit to disarm the citizens of the Non/less then lethal weapons' by illegalizing batons and brass knuckles and regulating Rubber Bullets, Knives, Pepper Spray/Mace and forcing a Concealed Pistol License for Stun Guns and Tazers even if open carried, if elected i will fight for your 2nd Amendment right, I will not stand by and do nothing while the citizens of Michigan are stripped of their defenses and fed to the snakes.

I will fight to create Legislation for Police Self-Insurance.

One of many things empowering the Criminals within the police is that You the taxpayer have to pay for all the unlawful conduct. I will create and fight to pass legislation for Police Self-Insurance, This will force the police to use the provided taxpayers funds, already given to them annually which is allocated to them by the state to pay all court and legal fees, including all fines, lawsuits and settlements if found at fault instead of forcing the taxpayers to pay Extra in a Frivolous and Unnecessary Taxes.

I will fight to reduce bureaucratic barriers for small businesses and vendors.

I am committed to reducing bureaucratic barriers and streamlining regulations to support and empower small businesses, fostering economic growth and job creation within our community.
